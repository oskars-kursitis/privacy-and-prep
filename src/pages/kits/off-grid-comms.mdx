title: Off-Grid Comms Kit (Urban)
description: Keep talking when cell service is overloaded or down.
---

### Objective
Short-range team comms + long-range info without relying on the phone network.

### Loadout (must-have)
- **Walkie-talkie pair** (license-free) or **BaoFeng + license**.  
  [Simple set](/go/walkie-set/) • [BaoFeng](/go/baofeng/)
- **Bridgefy/Briar apps** — Bluetooth/mesh text when data is down.  
  [Bridgefy](/go/bridgefy/) • [Briar](/go/briar-app/)
- **Emergency radio** — news & weather. [Get](/go/emergency-radio/)
- **Headset/earpiece** — hear in noisy areas. [Get](/go/earplugs/)
- **Power bank + solar panel** — keep radios/phones alive. [Bank](/go/power-bank/) • [Solar](/go/solar-panel/)

### Privacy & travel add-ons
- **Hardware security key** for accounts. [Get](/go/hardware-key/)
- **Privacy screen** for shoulder-surfing. [Get](/go/privacy-screen/)
- **Faraday pouch** (optional) to limit tracking. [Get](/go/faraday-pouch/)

### Notes
Pre-program common local channels. Agree on a **primary**, **backup**, and **check-in time** with your group. Keep a paper list of contacts.
