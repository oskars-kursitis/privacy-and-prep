---
title: Car Emergency Kit (All-Season)
description: Breakdown, storm, or traffic shutdown—stay warm, visible, and mobile.
---

### Objective
Survive an overnight stuck vehicle and reach help if needed.

### Loadout (must-have)
- **LED lantern / headlamp** — work light + hazard visibility. [Lantern](/go/led-lantern/) • [Headlamp](/go/headlamp/)
- **Thermal blankets (x2)** — heat retention. [Get](/go/thermal-blanket/)
- **Water bottles (2L)** + **EDC bottle** for refills. [Bottle](/go/edc-bottle/)
- **Power bank (20,000 mAh)** — phone + hazard lights. [Get](/go/power-bank/)
- **Mini first-aid kit** — bleeding & meds. [Get](/go/mini-fak/)
- **Multitool** — basic fixes. [Get](/go/multitool/)
- **Comms** — radio/walkies in convoy. [Walkie set](/go/walkie-set/)
- **High-vis & glow sticks** — mark vehicle at night (use lantern flash if needed).

### Cold-weather add
- **Thermal hat & gloves**, spare socks.
- **Emergency ration bars** (non-melting).

### Optional upgrades
- **Compact stove** (for hot drinks outside the car). [Stove](/go/camping-stove/)
- **Water filter** for longer events. [Filter](/go/water-filter/)

### Notes
Keep the kit in a soft tote in the trunk. Charge the bank monthly. In snow: clear exhaust pipe before idling for heat.
